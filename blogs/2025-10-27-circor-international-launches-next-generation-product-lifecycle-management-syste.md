CIRCOR International Launches Next-Generation Product Lifecycle Management System at Leslie Controls jennifer 2025-10-27 - 16:05 Leslie Controls Press Releases 27.10.2025 Burlington, MA – October 27, 2025 — CIRCOR International, Inc., a leading manufacturer of flow and motion control products for the world’s most severe-service applications, today announced a significant digital transformation milestone: the successful go-live of its new Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) system, Aras Innovator , at its Leslie Controls business. This milestone marks the culmination of months of cross-functional collaboration by the Integrated Product Team at Leslie Controls, which worked to consolidate a complex network of legacy systems into a single, unified PLM platform. The new system will serve as a digital backbone for product data and engineering processes, transforming how CIRCOR designs, builds, and supports its Navy product portfolio. “This achievement reflects the power of teamwork and vision,” said Bryan Reimers, VP/General Manager, Leslie Controls . “It’s about giving our people the tools they need to work smarter and deliver excellence for our customers. With this foundation in place, we’re now positioned to enable a true digital thread and future digital twin capabilities, improving traceability, accelerating innovation, and enhancing product quality.” By implementing Aras Innovator, CIRCOR gains a scalable platform that empowers its engineering, operations, supply chain, IT, and program management teams to collaborate more efficiently. The system is designed to improve data accuracy, streamline workflows, and enable faster, more informed decision-making, while supporting long-term Navy program requirements. “This transformation isn’t just a technology upgrade, it’s a leap forward in how we connect data, decisions, and teams across the product lifecycle,” said Krishna Kashyap, SVP, Chief Information Officer . CIRCOR recognizes the dedication and leadership of the project team: David Lieske, Kenneth Ballash, Eric Miller, Amy Peguese, Jason Carpenter, David Young, Vicky Vescio, and Kiera Jones , whose expertise and commitment drove the successful launch. About CIRCOR International, Inc. Founded in 1999, CIRCOR International, Inc. designs, manufactures and markets differentiated flow control products and sub-systems for Aerospace & Defense and Industrial customers. The Company has a diversified product portfolio of recognized, market-leading brands (Allweiler®, Houttuin™, Imo®, Hale Hamilton, Aerodyne Controls, CIRCOR Aerospace, RTK®, Schroedahl®, Tushaco®, Warren® and Zenith®) that fulfill its customers’ mission critical needs. CIRCOR markets its solutions directly and through various sales partners to more than 14,000 customers in approximately 100 countries. The Company has a global presence with approximately 3,000 employees and is headquartered in Burlington, Massachusetts. For more information, visit the Company’s website at http://circor.com .
